VIBES

Exclusivity and Excellence are the driving forces behind the Elektrik Eel Festival, it has engineered a vibe that is tailor fit to the underground house scene.

Elektrik Eel is focused on creating an environment that appreciates premium artists, unforgettable moments and electrifying music.

BECOME AN EEL

The Artists we are showcasing are some of the best in the world so make sure to keep an ear out for your favourite artist!

Each Elektrik Eel Festival is located underneath the nightlife of the city, that level of isolation makes the music insanely clear and shockingly loud. THE WAY WE LIKE IT!

BECOME ELEKTRIFIED

Away from the music, there are one of a kind art pieces on every wall, there is a photo booth and a few secrets that you have to find! Hint: Find the Golden Eel.

With every dancing fit, there should be a place to relax, at every venue has a place palace to rest your legs and chill out for a bit, eat a bit and maybe charge your phone so you can capture capture more memories.
